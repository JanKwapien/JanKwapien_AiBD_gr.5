Metadata
year 		- Year of release
artist.inverted - Surename and name of song author
track 		- Title of the song
time		- Length of the song
genre		- Genre of the song
date.entered	- Date when song entered top charts
date.peaked 	- Date when song was the most popular on the charts
xNth.week 	- where N is a natural number, place in top charts in Nth week since entering
